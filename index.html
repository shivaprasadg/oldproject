Kempegowda I was the founder of Bangalore in 1537 AD. Early name was Bendha Kaalooru [Bendha Kaalooru means Boiled Beans in Kannda] [ooru means City]. Later it became Bengaluru in Kannada Bangalore in English. The present city was designed by Kempe Gowda. Kempegowda built a mud fort in 1537 and with the help of vijayanagar Kingdom King Achutaraya, he built the little towns of Balepet, Cottonpet and Chickpet inside the fort. kempegowda built a four The shahajirao Bhonsle Father of Shivaji rao captured the city in 1638. And later on in 1687 Aurangazeb army capture the city.Then they sold Bangalore to Mysore Wodeyar. Wadeyar built Lalbagh in the same year. after the death of Krishnaraja Wodeyar II king of Mysore Wodeyar, Hyder Ali who was the commander- chief in Wodeyar's Army took the incharge of City. After Hyder Ali his son Tippu Sultan Tiger of Mysore took the incharge. Tippu sultan built Tippu summer palace in the heart of city K R Mareket. Tippu Sultan was defeated by British in the Fourth Anglo-Mysore War 1799. British took complete control of Mysore kingdom. British shifted administrative capital to Bangalore from Srirangapatna.The Post Office and Cantonment was established nine years later in 1809. Under the British influence, Bangalore bloomed with modern facilities like the railways, telegraph, postal and police departments. In the year 1859 British introduce First Train to Banagalore, They built Cubbon Park and Sankey.
